## 18. Final Submission Summary

Before submitting, make sure your story is clear:

```text
Problem:
Innovation ecosystems manually verify startups and mentors, causing repeated work, inconsistent decisions, and weak linkages.

Solution:
TrustPass AI turns existing evidence into verified digital passports with stamps and badges.

AI:
Gemini-powered agents autofill profiles, verify eligibility/readiness, detect risks, recommend stamps, and suggest ecosystem linkages.

Human Governance:
Admins review and approve passport issuance, stamps, and linkages.

Impact:
Faster verification, reusable trust, smarter ecosystem coordination, and better access to support.
```
