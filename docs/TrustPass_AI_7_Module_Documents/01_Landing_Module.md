# TrustPass AI — Module 1: Landing Module

## 1. Module Purpose

The **Landing Module** is the public-facing entry point of TrustPass AI.

Its job is to explain the product clearly, create trust, and guide different visitors into the right journey:

- Startup founder → start verification
- Mentor → become verified mentor
- Programme admin → request demo or log in
- Ecosystem owner → understand business value
- Partner / service provider → understand how they can participate

This module should make TrustPass AI feel like a serious digital trust infrastructure product, not just another startup management dashboard.

---

## 2. Core Message

The landing experience should communicate one clear idea:

> **TrustPass AI issues digital passports for startups and mentors, helping innovation ecosystems verify once and connect everywhere.**

The visitor should understand within 10 seconds:

1. What the product does
2. Who it is for
3. Why it matters
4. What action to take next

---

## 3. Main User Types on Landing Page

## 3.1 Startup Founder

A founder visits the site to get their startup verified.

They should immediately understand:

- They can create a Startup Passport
- They can upload startup evidence
- They may receive stamps and badges
- Their passport can help them access programmes, mentors, and partners

## 3.2 Mentor

A mentor visits to become part of the ecosystem.

They should understand:

- They can receive a Mentor Passport
- Their expertise can be verified
- They can receive relevant startup linkage requests
- Their mentor history and credibility can be represented through stamps

## 3.3 Programme Administrator

An admin wants to reduce manual verification work.

They should understand:

- TrustPass AI helps verify applicants faster
- It creates consistent AI-assisted review
- Human approval remains in control
- The system recommends linkages after verification

## 3.4 Ecosystem Owner

A government agency, accelerator, university, or corporate innovation team wants ecosystem scale.

They should understand:

- Passport credentials can be reused across programmes
- Verification intelligence is not lost
- Ecosystem actors become easier to trust and coordinate
- Reporting and governance become easier

## 5.2 Problem Section

This section explains the current pain.

### Message

Innovation ecosystems still verify people manually.

Admins manually check:

- Startup applications
- Founder details
- Pitch decks
- Mentor profiles
- Programme eligibility
- Supporting evidence
- Readiness
- Matching suitability

### Key Pain Cards

| Pain | Explanation |
|---|---|
| Manual Verification | Admins spend hours checking applications and documents |
| Repeated Checks | Startups and mentors repeat the same verification across programmes |
| Inconsistent Review | Different admins may evaluate the same profile differently |
| Lost Trust History | Past verification and engagement data is not reused |
| Weak Linkages | Poor verification leads to poor mentor, partner, and programme matching |

---

## 5.3 Solution Section

This section introduces the product.

### Message

TrustPass AI converts manual verification into reusable digital passports.

### Flow Visual

```text
Apply → AI Verify → Human Review → Passport Issued → Stamps Earned → Linkages Activated
```

### Explanation

When a startup or mentor is verified, they receive a digital passport issued by TrustPass AI. The passport contains badges, stamps, verification status, and a shareable proof link.

This passport can then be reused across programmes, mentors, partners, and regions.

## 5.5 How It Works Section

Use 5 steps.

| Step | Title | Explanation |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Submit Profile | Startup or mentor submits profile and evidence |
| 2 | AI Verification | AI checks identity, eligibility, readiness, risk, and missing evidence |
| 3 | Human Review | Admin reviews AI explanation and approves or requests evidence |
| 4 | Passport Issued | User receives a digital Startup or Mentor Passport |
| 5 | Linkages Activated | Passport unlocks relevant programmes, mentors, partners, or startups |

## 5.7 Responsible AI Section

This section is important because verification affects access to opportunities.

### Message

```text
AI recommends. Humans decide.
```

### Key Points

- AI does not reject users automatically
- Admins approve final decisions
- Every recommendation includes explanation
- Users can submit missing evidence
- Risk flags are review signals, not final judgments
- Passport status can be renewed, updated, or revoked by authorised admins

## 11. Success Criteria

The Landing Module is successful if:

- Visitors understand the product in under 10 seconds
- The passport concept feels unique and memorable
- Startups know how to begin verification
- Mentors know how to get verified
- Admins understand the business value
- The product feels enterprise-ready
- The interface does not look like a basic admin dashboard
